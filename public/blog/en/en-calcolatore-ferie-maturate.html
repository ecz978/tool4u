Frequently Asked Questions

How many vacation days should I earn monthly?

Standard full-time employment typically accrues vacation rights at a rate of approximately two to two and a half days per month, depending on national labor laws and seniority. Part-time contracts usually prorate this amount proportionally.

Can I carry over unused days to the next year?

Rollover policies vary significantly by employer and contract type. Many jurisdictions mandate that unused leave be paid out upon termination, but active employment often requires usage within the current calendar year. Always verify your specific company handbook.

Does unpaid leave affect my accrual rate?

Generally, extended periods of unpaid absence pause vacation accumulation. Most calculations only count actively worked months or paid leave periods. The online calculator focuses on active employment duration to ensure compliance with standard payroll rules.

What should I do if my calculation differs from my payslip?

Discrepancies can occur due to different start dates, unpaid sick leave adjustments, or company-specific rounding rules. Compare your inputs with the exact pay periods listed on your official documentation. If differences persist, request an audit from your human resources department.
