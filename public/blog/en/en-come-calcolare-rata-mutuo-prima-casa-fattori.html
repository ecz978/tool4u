Understanding Mortgage Rates: The Key Factors

Mortgage rates are influenced by several key factors, including:

  • Credit Score: Your credit score plays a significant role in determining the interest rate you'll qualify for. A higher credit score can result in a lower interest rate.
  • Down Payment: The amount you put down can affect the interest rate. A larger down payment can lead to a lower interest rate.
  • Loan Term: The length of the loan can impact the interest rate. A shorter loan term can result in a lower interest rate.
  • Property Type: The type of property you're purchasing can also influence the interest rate.
