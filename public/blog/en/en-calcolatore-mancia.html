Frequently Asked Questions

Does the calculator work with different global currencies?

Yes, the system operates purely on numerical values. You enter the exact amount from your receipt, and the math applies accurately regardless of whether you are paying in euros, dollars, pounds, or any other fiat currency.

What if our group ordered items at vastly different price points?

The standard function assumes an equal split, which is the fastest method to resolve a shared check. If individual orders vary significantly, calculate separate subtotals independently on the tool first, then add them together to apply your final gratuity percentage.

Should tipping be calculated on the pre-tax or post-tax total?

Standard etiquette strictly recommends applying gratuity to your pre-tax subtotal. Adding a percentage after sales tax has been applied results in overcompensating, since tax revenue goes to local governments and not your server.
