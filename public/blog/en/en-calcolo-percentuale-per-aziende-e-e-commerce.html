What is Percentage Calculation?

Percentage calculation is the process of finding a part of a whole as a proportion of 100. It's a crucial metric for businesses and e-commerce sites, as it helps you understand your sales, profits, and customer behavior. By calculating percentages, you can identify trends, spot opportunities, and make informed decisions about your business.
