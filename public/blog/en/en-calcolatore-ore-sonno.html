Practical Tips for Improving Your Sleep

In addition to using our sleep calculator tool, there are several other tips that can help you improve the quality of your sleep. These include:

  • Establishing a consistent sleep schedule and sticking to it, even on weekends
  • Creating a relaxing bedtime routine, such as reading a book or taking a warm bath
  • Avoiding caffeine and electronic devices before bedtime
  • Creating a sleep-conducive environment, such as keeping the room cool, dark, and quiet
