What Is Placeholder Text?

Placeholder text, commonly known as dummy text or Lorem Ipsum, is temporary content used in design projects. It serves multiple purposes: it helps visualize layouts, test typography, ensure proper spacing, and demonstrate how content will flow on a page. Rather than waiting for final content, designers and developers use this standardized text to work efficiently.

The most popular form is Lorem Ipsum, Latin-derived text that's been used since the 1500s. Modern alternatives include plain English text, character-specific options, and customizable formats.

Practical Tips for Using Placeholder Text

Best Practices

When incorporating placeholder text into your projects, follow these guidelines:

  • Match content length — Generate enough text to represent final content volume
  • Use realistic formatting — Include headings, paragraphs, and line breaks
  • Test readability — Ensure fonts and sizes work with actual text length
  • Mark as temporary — Clearly identify placeholder areas for team members
  • Plan replacement — Set timelines for swapping real content before launch

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Lorem Ipsum and English placeholder text?
Lorem Ipsum is pseudo-Latin text that's been standard since the 1500s, making it ideal for design mockups because it doesn't distract with actual meaning. English placeholder text uses real words randomly arranged, better for testing readability and ensuring fonts work well with actual language patterns.
Can I use placeholder text in production websites?
No, placeholder text should never appear on live websites. It's strictly for design, development, and testing phases. Always replace it with final, original content before publishing. Search engines and users expect real, meaningful content on published pages.
How much placeholder text should I generate?
Generate slightly more text than you expect your final content to be. If you're planning 500 words of actual content, generate 600-700 words of placeholder. This ensures your design accommodates the real content properly without unexpected layout shifts.
Is placeholder text copyrighted?
Standard Lorem Ipsum and generic placeholder text are not copyrighted and are free to use in any project. However, some creative variations may have specific licenses. Always check the specific generator's terms, though most placeholder text is freely available for both personal and commercial use.
Can placeholder text generators create specific formats?
Yes, most modern generators offer multiple format options including paragraphs, lists, headings, and even structured data. You can specify exactly how many paragraphs you need, how long each should be, and what format best suits your project requirements.
