Practical Tips for Fair Expense Management

1. Calculate Immediately

Run the calculator right at the moment of payment, while the receipt is fresh. This prevents people from forgetting details or estimating amounts incorrectly later.

2. Account for Beverages Separately

If some friends ordered alcohol and others didn't, consider splitting just the meal equally and having drinkers pay additional for their beverages. Our tool handles this custom breakdown easily.

3. Include Gratuity Upfront

Add tips to the total before splitting. This ensures everyone contributes fairly to service charges and prevents post-calculation disputes about appropriate tip percentages.

4. Document Everything

Keep a screenshot or note of the split results. If questions arise later, you have documented proof of who agreed to what amounts.

5. Settle Quickly

Don't let debts accumulate over time. Address payments within a day or two while everyone remembers the occasion and details remain fresh.
