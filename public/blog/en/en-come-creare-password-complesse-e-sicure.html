Learn How to Create Complex and Secure Passwords

In today's digital age, password security is more important than ever. With the increasing number of online accounts and sensitive data, it's essential to create complex and secure passwords to safeguard your online identity and protect your data from unauthorized access.

But What Makes a Strong Password?

A strong password should be a minimum of 12 characters long, contain a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. It should also be easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess.

Practical Tips for Creating Secure Passwords

Here are some practical tips to help you create secure passwords:

  1. Use a password manager to generate and store unique passwords for each of your online accounts.
  2. Avoid using easily guessable information such as your name, birthdate, or common words.
  3. Don't reuse passwords across multiple accounts.
  4. Change your passwords regularly, especially for sensitive accounts.
