Frequently Asked Questions

Can I adjust the interest rate for inflation?

Yes, always account for inflation to secure a realistic purchasing power estimate. If historical averages show returns around 7 percent, consider inputting a conservative 4 percent net rate to reflect rising costs over decades.

Does the calculator handle variable market conditions?

The platform assumes a fixed annual percentage rate for simplicity. To simulate real-world volatility, run multiple scenarios using different percentages, such as 3 percent, 6 percent, and 9 percent, to establish a realistic outcome range.

Is this tool suitable for debt planning as well?

Absolutely. While designed primarily for asset growth, the same exponential formula applies inversely to liabilities. By entering a loan balance and credit card percentage, you can visualize how quickly unpaid amounts escalate when only minimum payments are made.
