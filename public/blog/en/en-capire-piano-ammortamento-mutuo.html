How Does a Mortgage Repayment Schedule Work?

A mortgage repayment schedule is a plan that outlines how you will repay your mortgage loan. It typically involves making regular payments, usually monthly, to the lender. Each payment consists of two main components: principal and interest.

The principal amount is the amount you borrowed to purchase the house, while the interest is the cost of borrowing that amount.

Practical Tips for Managing Your Mortgage Repayment Schedule

  • Make extra payments when possible: Making extra payments can help reduce your loan balance and save you money on interest.
  • Consider refinancing: Refinancing your mortgage can help you save money on interest and reduce your monthly payments.
  • Review and adjust your budget: Regularly reviewing and adjusting your budget can help you ensure that you have enough money to make your mortgage payments.
