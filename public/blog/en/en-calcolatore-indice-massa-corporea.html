Introduction to Body Mass Index (BMI)

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely used measure to assess weight status and health risks associated with being underweight, overweight, or obese. Calculating your BMI can help you understand your body composition and take necessary steps to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

How Our BMI Calculator Works

Our BMI calculator is a simple and easy-to-use tool that requires you to input your weight and height. The calculator then uses the BMI formula (weight in kg / height in meters squared) to determine your BMI score. You can use this score to categorize your weight status as underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Practical Tips for Maintaining a Healthy BMI

Here are some practical tips to help you maintain a healthy BMI:

  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
  • Engage in regular physical activity, such as walking, running, or swimming
  • Monitor your weight and adjust your diet and exercise routine accordingly
